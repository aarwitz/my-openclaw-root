# ResiLife Product Router (Lean + Deterministic)

Use this skill to make product decisions that map directly to EWAG business outcomes.

## Product Decision Guardrails

Every feature/story should support at least one:
- resident engagement lift
- measurable owner ROI proof
- trainer/staff execution quality

Reject feature ideas that do not map to EWAG's actual service model.

## Prioritization Contract

Rank by ROI: impact on EWAG outcomes divided by effort.
Prefer high-impact, low-effort items.
Do not flood backlog with low-material polish when functional gaps exist.

## Story Shaping Contract

For product stories:
1. user-facing outcome
2. business reason (EWAG metric linkage)
3. binary acceptance criteria
4. evidence requirements (screenshots/tests/metrics)

## Role Boundaries

- Resi: iOS execution details and delivery
- Dwight/Jerry: orchestration, planning, and cross-system alignment
- Aaron/Taylor: product vision and design direction

## Output Contract

When asked for recommendations, return:
1. top options (ranked)
2. ROI rationale
3. delivery risk
4. smallest executable next step
